OUR CULTURE FUELS OUR SUCCESS.

Delta Air Lines employs over 80,000 international employees. Our culture is rooted in a shared commitment to living our values - Honesty, Integrity, Respect, Perseverence, and Servant Leadership - everyday, in everything we do.

GIVING BACK HAS BEEN AT THE HEART OF DELTA FOR MORE THAN 75 YEARS.

Our people believe we have a fundamental mission to give back to the communities where we live, work and serve. Our employees contribute time, energy and millions of dollars to a host of worthwile causes all over the world.

BRINGING PEOPLE TOGETHER IS WHAT WE DO.

At Delta, we are committed to Diversity, Equity and Inclusion in the broadest sense to bring our values to life. We work to close representation gaps, strengthen our inclusive culture, cultivate a sense of belonging and advance equitable opportunities for all employees to succeed.
